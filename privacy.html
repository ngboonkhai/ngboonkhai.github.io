Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how ngboonkhai.github.io ("the Site") handles information when you visit or use the tools provided here.

The tools on this site (32-Bit Manipulation Tool, Email Quote Appender) run entirely in your browser. No data you enter into any tool is transmitted to or stored on any server. All processing happens locally on your device.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect certain technical data (such as IP addresses) as part of their standard server logging. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

Advertising (Google AdSense)

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

This site itself does not set cookies beyond those used by Google AdSense (described above) and browser sessionStorage to remember which tool you last had open (this data never leaves your browser).

Third-Party Links

The site links to external resources such as GitHub repositories. This Privacy Policy does not cover third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
